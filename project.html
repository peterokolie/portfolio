project idea:

in the summer of 2021, we decided to create a problem for people to
be able to get stuff done from home. Hair-making, nail technician and
so forth. i met and partnered with Chris, another UX/UI designer who
showed me the ropes and together we were able to brainstorm and
come up with an idea.

The goal is to find a way to make things easy for people
especially with Covid-19 changing the way the world would normally
work thereby forcing people to stay andwork from home.

the problem

Beginning 2021, many people were still working from home due to Covid and so far,
it seemed like this was gonna be the normal. it made me begin to think
of many ways we can help bring services to them from their homes. it birthed
this idea to create a platform where people can be able to get beauty
specialist from home, the target were mostly women and girls.

the task to be completed

This platform should enable users to:

  • Register an account
  • Contact workers
  • Message workers via in app chatbox
  • Schedule appointments
  • Confirm workers arrival
  • Confirm that job have been done
  • Confirm payment has been made

proposed solution

Tradservis-an app that allow workers to be able to contact
workers and communicate with them, book appointments, and
arrange meet-up

it also double as a job posting website for people in the beauty
industry to be able to get jobs and customers.

the design

we mainly focused on the MVP while working on our design. we focused on simplicity and great user experience.
